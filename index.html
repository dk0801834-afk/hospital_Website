Meet Dr. Anu Bhandari

Dr. Anu Bhandari MBBS, DGO

Providing Specialized Obstetric & Gynecological Solutions Since 2008

Dr. Anu Bhandari is an esteemed Gynecologist and Obstetrician in Ludhiana, Punjab. With over 18 years of clinical and surgical expertise, she has helped thousands of families transition smoothly into parenthood. She is highly regarded for her clinical acumen, dedication to normal deliveries, and specialized protocols in handling complex infertility cases.

Academic Credentials & Certifications

  • MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery) Kasturba Medical College, Manipal University (Batch of 2006)
  • DGO (Diploma in Gynecology and Obstetrics) Medical College, Calcutta (Batch of 2008)
  • Active Member of Medical Council Indian Medical Association (IMA) & Ludhiana Obstetric & Gynecological Society
What We Excel In

Our Clinical Specialties

Maternity & Normal Delivery

Complete prenatal care, routine check-ups, normal delivery coaching, high-risk pregnancy monitoring, and comprehensive postnatal rehabilitation plans.

Infertility & IVF Support

Advanced infertility diagnostic investigations, counseling, Ovulation Induction, IUI (Intrauterine Insemination), and IVF program coordination.

Endoscopic & Laparoscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive keyhole surgeries for ovarian cysts, fibroids, uterine polyps, laparoscopic hysterectomy, and tubal patency tests with rapid recovery.

Gynecological Disorder Care

Effective treatment for irregular menstruation, painful periods, PCOD/PCOS clinical management, menopause counseling, and pelvic infections.

Pediatric & Well-Baby Care

Complete new-born health assessment, guidance for post-maternity baby care, breast-feeding support, and routine baby vaccination packages.

Diagnostics & Lab Pharmacy

In-house laboratory investigations for prompt reports (pregnancy tests, blood profiles, hormone panels) and standard fully stocked pharmacy counter.
